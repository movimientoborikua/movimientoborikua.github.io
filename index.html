Welcome

Movimiento Borikua

will serve and unite Puerto Ricans in the diaspora, focusing our efforts in the Phoenix metropolitan area, with plans to expand as the organization’s reach and influence increases throughout the state. primary efforts will include: Organizing civic engagement events that educate participants on the history of Puerto Rico, inspiring individuals to become politically engaged. Growing a Puerto Rican-led youth movement to demonstrate a vision for what being Puerto Rican on and off the island could be. Tackling issues important to Puerto Ricans moving from the Island to the Diaspora.
